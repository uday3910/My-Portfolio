Projects

Room Tracker

The Room Tracker App is a user-friendly application designed to streamline the management of college rooms efficiently. It is specifically tailored for college administrations to monitor and manage the allocation, availability, and utilization of various rooms within the college premises. This mini project aims to provide a digital solution to the traditional paper-based room booking systems, enhancing accuracy, accessibility, and convenience.The app maintains a comprehensive inventory of all available rooms within the college campus, categorizing them based on their type (e.g., classrooms, laboratories, conference rooms).Each room entry includes details such as room number, capacity, facilities available, and any specific requirements or restrictions.



Health care management


The Health Care Management Mini Project is a software application designed to streamline and optimize various aspects of healthcare management within a hospital or healthcare facility. This project aims to address common challenges faced by healthcare administrators, healthcare providers, and patients by providing a comprehensive digital solution.The application allows healthcare providers to maintain electronic health records (EHR) for patients, including personal details, medical history, diagnoses, medications, and treatment plans. Patients can register and schedule appointments through the application, reducing waiting times and administrative burdens.Users can schedule appointments with healthcare providers based on availability, specialty, and location.

Internet Service Provider

The Internet service provider (ISP) database project using SQL is designed to manage various aspects of ISP operations efficiently. It includes tables for storing customer information such as names, addresses, and contact details. Additionally, the database tracks subscription plans, billing information, and service usage for each customer. SQL queries facilitate tasks like generating invoices, monitoring bandwidth usage, and managing customer accounts. The database also supports administrative functions such as adding new services, updating customer records, and analyzing usage trends. Ultimately, the ISP database project enhances service delivery, billing accuracy, and customer satisfaction through streamlined data management and retrieval.
